Exercise 1: matching floating numbers
1. ^-?\d+(,\d+)*(\.\d+(e\d+)?)?$
Exercise 2: matching phone numbers
2. (^\d{3})|((?<=\()\d+)|(\d+\d{2})
Exercise 3: matching emails
3. (^(\w*\.\w*))|(^(\w*))
Exercise 4: matching HTML tags
4. ^\<(\w*)
Exercise 5: matching files
5. ^(\w+)\.(jpg|png|gif)$
Exercise 6: matching empty strings and endings
6. ^\s*(.*)$
Exercise 7: getting data from log files
7. (\w+)\(([\w\.]+):(\d+)\)
Exercise 8: getting data from URLs
8. ^(\w+)://([\w\-\.]+)(:(\d+))?
